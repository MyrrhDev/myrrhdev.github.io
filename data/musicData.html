Studies into Music: Song Features and Lyrics

Songs are made both of music and lyrics, my plan is to run both studies. First, in part I focusing on the song features and part II exploring the lyrics. Lastly, a comparative study to see which is most likely to be a better predictive model for mood (happy, sad, angry, relaxed). 1) Descriptive analysis of my song features 2) Data Transformation 3) Another dataset, gathering their features and cleaning a larger dataset 4) Training new larger dataset model based on features: Supervised learning (Decision Tree & Random Forest)
